========================================
   HOW TO SHARE YOUR BIRTHDAY WEBSITE
   (Without Hosting)
========================================

OPTION 1: SHARE VIA GOOGLE DRIVE / ONEDRIVE / DROPBOX
------------------------------------------------------
1. ZIP the entire "Birthday" folder
   - Right-click the folder ‚Üí "Compress to ZIP file"

2. Upload the ZIP to:
   - Google Drive: drive.google.com
   - OneDrive: onedrive.com
   - Dropbox: dropbox.com

3. Get shareable link:
   - Right-click the ZIP file ‚Üí "Get link" / "Share"
   - Set to "Anyone with link can view"
   - Copy the link

4. Send the link to your customer!

5. Customer instructions:
   - Download the ZIP
   - Extract/Unzip the folder
   - Open "index.html" in browser (Chrome/Edge/Firefox)


OPTION 2: SHARE VIA WHATSAPP / TELEGRAM
---------------------------------------
1. ZIP the entire "Birthday" folder
2. Send the ZIP file directly in chat
3. Customer downloads, extracts, opens index.html


OPTION 3: SHARE VIA EMAIL
-------------------------
1. ZIP the entire "Birthday" folder
2. Attach ZIP to email (if under 25MB)
3. Or upload to Google Drive and share link in email
